# Stage 5 — Choose Your Feature Track

## Today's goal
Add one significant new feature to the app — chosen based on your group's pace and interest.

---

## The three tracks

Your mentor will tell you which track the group is doing today. Each track adds a different feature to the app.

---

### Track A — Photo Attachment
**Add photos to entries.**
When creating an entry, the user can pick a photo from the gallery. A thumbnail is shown on the entry card.

→ See [stage-5-track-a.md](stage-5-track-a.md)

---

### Track B — Search & Discovery
**Search through entries.**
A search bar appears on the home screen. As the user types, the feed filters to show only matching entries. A "no results" state is shown when nothing matches.

→ See [stage-5-track-b.md](stage-5-track-b.md)

---

### Track C — Daily Reminder
**Set a daily reminder to add a memory.**
A Settings screen lets the user turn on/off a daily notification. The notification appears at a chosen time each day.

→ See [stage-5-track-c.md](stage-5-track-c.md)

---

## Checkpoint outcome

Whichever track you do, at the end of the day the app should have a **working, visible new feature** that you can demonstrate. It doesn't need to be perfect — it needs to work.
